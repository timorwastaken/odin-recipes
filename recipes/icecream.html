Ice Cream Recipe

Description

This strawberry ice cream is rich, creamy, and full of fresh and fruity flavor.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Milk and Cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla and Salt
  • Food Coloring

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine strawberries, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, salt, and food coloring in a large bowl.
  3. Pour strawberry mixture into the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker
  4. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm
