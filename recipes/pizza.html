Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 package dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablesppons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place warm water in a bowl
  3. Add flour, oil and yeast
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes
